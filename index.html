Why UpTick ?

BEWARE - No representative of UpTick Trading will EVER contact you first, or offer investments of any kind.

I've experienced the live markets. I have endured the losses, blown accounts and have had the successes making my first $100,000 at just 18 Years Old. Though with the forex markets it's as easy to put that money back in plus more - IF you don't treat this industry with the right discipline or the correct techniques you need to become a consistent Forex Trader and I’m talking from 7 years of experience.

(and yes I lost the 100k)

There are many ways to trade the market; as long as you're profitable. But there’s a difference from making profit and doing it year in year out.

Our vision was to be able to build a trading community which aims to focus on the psychological, technical (strategy) and executional parts which make up Trading in its entirety. Trading isn’t all sunshine and rainbows. It’s hundreds - thousands of hours of work. However, UpTick will build a successful community driven organisation to show the reasons you should trade the Forex Markets.

UpTick Trading is Educational and Propetity trading org. Located in Essex. Tommy Taylor, a Trader with 7 years of experience has helped people get funding and mentor those who want to take their trading to the next level.

Alongside Tommy, we have 3 others running UpTick from our trading floor. Putting us in a great position to teach our strategy, help you master the charts and then push you to become a consistently profitable retail trader.
